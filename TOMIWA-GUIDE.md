# Tomanni Wear — Studio Guide for Tomiwa

Welcome to the Tomanni Wear backend. This is where you manage all the products on the site.

---

## How to access the Studio

Go to: **https://tomanni-wear.sanity.studio**  
Log in with your Google account (tomiwamusa111@gmail.com).

You'll see a panel on the left with **Products** and **Site Settings**.

---

## Adding a new product

1. Click **Products** in the left panel
2. Click the **+** button (top right) to create a new product
3. Fill in the fields:
   - **Name** — the product name that appears on the site
   - **Slug** — click *Generate* and it creates the URL automatically (e.g. `skeleton-hoodie`)
   - **Price (₦)** — the price the customer pays. Enter numbers only, no symbols (e.g. `45000`)
   - **Main Image** — upload the product photo
   - **Description** — short description of the product
   - **Category** — choose Men, Women, or Accessories
   - **Available Sizes** — tick the sizes you have in stock
   - **Show in New Arrivals** — tick this to make the product appear on the homepage carousel
   - **In Stock** — leave this ticked. Untick it to show a "Sold Out" label
4. Click **Publish** (top right) — the product goes live on the site immediately

## Putting a product on sale

This is how sales work on Tomanni Wear:

**Normal product:**
- **Price**: 45000
- **Compare-at Price**: *(leave empty)*
- Site shows: ₦45,000

**Product on sale:**
- **Price**: 32000 ← lower the price to the sale price
- **Compare-at Price**: 45000 ← put the original price here
- Site shows: ₦32,000 ~~₦45,000~~ · 29% off

**To end a sale:**
- Delete the number in **Compare-at Price** (leave it blank)
- Change **Price** back to the original price
- Click **Publish**

That's it. No switches to flip. If Compare-at Price is filled in and higher than Price, the site automatically shows the sale badge.

---

## Editing an existing product

1. Click **Products** in the left panel
2. Click the product you want to edit
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Publish** to save — changes go live immediately

---

## Changing the announcement bar

The black bar at the top of the site (e.g. "Free shipping on orders over ₦50,000").

1. Click **Site Settings** in the left panel
2. Edit the **Announcement Bar Text**
3. You can also untick **Show Announcement Bar** to hide it completely
4. Click **Publish**
